INTO THE DANISH WILD: A WILDLIFE ADVENTURE

Embark on a wildlife tour in Denmark and discover nature in its purest form. From coastal birds to forest deer, every moment brings a new wild encounter. Experience the beauty, tranquility, and wonder of Denmark’s untamed landscapes.





LEGOLAND® Billund Resort

Nordmarksvej 9, 7190 Billund, Denmark

Creativity is central to how we do things in Denmark - and that goes for family holidays too! At LEGOLAND® Billund Resort, which, by the way, is the original LEGOLAND®, you'll find lions on your doorstep, a house made of LEGO®, a mid-air playground and the chance to time travel, not to mention plenty of great places to stay and white sand beaches to discover.

Bakken Klampenborg

Dyrehavsbakken 207, 2930 Klampenborg, Denmark

Step into the world of fun and excitement at Bakken, the oldest amusement park in the world. From thrilling rides and lively attractions to cozy food stalls and charming gardens, there’s something for everyone in the family. Feel the adrenaline, laugh with friends, and make unforgettable memories in this magical destination just outside Copenhagen.

Sommerland Sjælland

Gl. Nykøbingvej 169, 4572 Nørre Asmindrup, Denmark

Dive into excitement at Sommerland Sjælland, Denmark’s ultimate family amusement and water park. From thrilling water slides and fun rides to relaxing green spaces and picnic spots, there’s adventure for all ages. Make unforgettable memories, laugh out loud, and enjoy a perfect day filled with sun, fun, and smiles.

WOW PARK Billund/Skjern

Ørneredevej 6, 8270 Højbjerg, Denmark

Unleash your sense of wonder at WOW PARK, an exciting outdoor adventure park perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike.
