FOR SOLO TRAVELER


Safe and bike-friendly cities Friendly locals and social culture Freedom to explore at your own pace




Budget List for a Solo Traveler in Denmark (Per Day)


  Accommodation
   Hostel dorm: €25–40
   Budget hotel / Airbnb (private): €60–100

  Food
   Street food / bakery meals: €8–15
   Casual café or budget restaurant: €15–25
   Estimated daily food budget: €25–40

  Transportation
   Public transport day pass: €10–15
   Bike rental (per day): €12–20
   Walking: Free

  Estimated daily transport budget: €10–20

  Attractions & Activities
   Museums / landmarks: €10–20
   Free attractions (parks, streets, churches): Free
   Free walking tours: Tip-based (€5–10)

  Estimated daily activities: €10–25

  Extras
   Coffee/snacks: €5–10
   Souvenirs/misc.: €5–15

  Estimated Total Daily Budget
   Budget traveler: €70–100 per day
   Mid-range solo traveler: €100–150 per day

  Budget Tips
   Use bikes and public transport
   Eat at food halls and bakeries
   Visit free museums and attractions
   Stay in hostels or shared Airbnbs
