CAMP, EXPLORE, AND RELAX: DENMARK'S BEST CAMPING DESTINATIONS

Camp, explore, and unwind in Denmark’s great outdoors!





Krusmølle Glamping in Southern Jutland

Viborgvej 2, 8000 Aarhus C, Denmark

Glamping does not always mean sleeping in a tent! Anyone who appreciates four walls on holiday will find what they are looking for at Krusmølle Glamping. Nine circus wagons have been renovated and converted into tasteful accommodation with a touch of Nordic design. Enjoy a glass of champagne from your private patio or on the nearby lake and watch the sun set behind the treetops.

'Wineglamping' in Randers

Haversmosevej 21, 8920 Randers NV, Denmarkk

If you want to try sleeping in a wine barrel, an airstream wagon, a luxury tent or similar, you should take the trip past Cold Hand Winery. Here you get a very unique experience and a different night’s sleep, and you don’t have to hold back during the wine tasting because you have to drive.

Tiki Camp in Fyn

Klavsebøllevej 16, 5953 Tranekær, Denmark

The concept behind Denmark's first "glamping site" is not just luxury and comfort, but truly being at one with nature. During a stay at the Tiki Camp take time for the best things in life, a little cosy cooking and eating in the open air, sitting together around the campfire, finally reading that book you've been meaning to finish or simply daydreaming. Very special hygge!

Eco Beach Camp on Bornholm

Vestre Strandvej 39, 3730 Nexø, Denmark

Is there actually anything that could make glamping even better? Yes, sea view glamping! This is exactly what Eco Beach Camp offers on the beautiful Baltic Sea island of Bornholm. With only 12 tents, the camp is not only Denmark's smallest campsite, but is also situated in the middle of the dunes on one of Denmark's best beaches, Balka Strand, in the south of the island.

Hegnet, Tåsinge

Himmelbjerget, 8680 Ry, Denmark

Wake up in the middle of unspoiled nature with a 180 degree ocean view, and feel yourself merge with nature. It's the perfect shelter for an overnight stay if you're cycling the N8 Baltic Sea route.
